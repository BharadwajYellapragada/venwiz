About Us

VenWiz is a unique technology platform to cater to E2E service partner requirements in any industrial set-up. We provide Service Partner Discovery, On-boarding, Management and Quality Assurance for a wide range of industries. For Service Partners, we provide the exposure to a larger customer base, standardized and easy transactions and access to tailor made skill sets and certifications.

Services

At VenWiz, we are determined to provide both industries and service providers with the best, streamlined access to each others.

Service Partner Discovery

For evey service requirement, we provide you with a simple curated set of vendors. Each service provider has a detailed description, work history, certifications received and rating given by previous clients. This makes the discovery of vendor effective and powerful, whether it be for asset maintainence or new tasks.

Service Partner Connect

With identification of service providers complete, we will help you get speedy invoices for your services.We will track the service that is provided. In case of deficiencies, we will feedback it back to the provider. Whats more, the provider will be rated based on the quality of the service provided.

For Service Providers

Too often are the service providers looking or mismatched for work. That's no more! We provide access to a large number of industries looking for the right service provider. For small and medium enterprises. We also provide a platform with automated ERP features and access to certifications and qualifications that will update your profile.
